01 Executive Summary

2024 was a significant year for Diginext's sustainability journey. We formalised our Net Zero commitment, achieved ISO 14644-8 classification compliance for cleanroom-adjacent data hall environments, and reduced our Scope 1 and 2 emissions by 18% year-on-year through a combination of fleet electrification and renewable energy procurement.

Our ESG programme is not a separate initiative — it is embedded in how we engineer, source materials, manage sites, and develop our people. Every project we deliver is assessed against our Environmental Impact Checklist before mobilisation.

ISO 14644-8:2013

Cleanroom Air Cleanliness by Chemical Concentration

ISO 14644-8 establishes the classification of air cleanliness by chemical concentration (ACC) for cleanrooms and associated controlled environments. Diginext operates in data hall environments where molecular contamination (AMC — Airborne Molecular Contamination) can impact sensitive hardware, optics, and long-term infrastructure reliability.

Our installation methodology for high-sensitivity environments (including hyperscale AI halls with precision optical interconnects) follows ISO 14644-8 guidance to minimise AMC risk during cabling and rack installation phases.
