Our Journey

Our Journey is a Shared Adventure.

From our founding in 2022 to active projects across 7 countries — every milestone reflects the dedication of our growing team and our commitment to excellence in data centre infrastructure.

Q3 2022

Founded in Sweden

Diginext Sweden AB founded in Stockholm, Sweden. Our founding team brought deep expertise in data centre cabling, fibre optic systems and structured infrastructure — setting out to build a Nordic-first engineering partner.

2023

Norway Expansion & First Major Contracts

Established Diginext Norway NUF with operations from our Stockholm headquarters. Secured first significant contracts in Oslo, Hamar and Kristiansand — delivering structured cabling and network equipment installation for major clients including TikTok and Global Direct.

2024

European Scale — 7 Countries

Delivered projects across Sweden, Norway, Poland, Portugal, England, Finland and Denmark. Key engagements included Microsoft (Gävle & Slough), AstraZeneca (Stockholm), IKEA (Slependen), CoreWeave (Falun & Kristiansand), and STACK Infrastructure (Stockholm). Completed 48 projects combining long-term and short-term collaborations.

2024–2025

114,000+ Safe Man-Hours

Achieved over 114,000 safe man-hours between 2024 and 2025 — reflecting our uncompromising commitment to occupational safety and operational excellence. Zero lost-time incidents across all active projects.
