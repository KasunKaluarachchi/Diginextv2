03 / Fibre Optic

High-integrity fibre optic — from design to commissioning.

Professional installation of fibre optic cables — both indoor and outdoor plant, from patch panel to campus backbone. We handle single-mode and multi-mode fibre across hyperscale and enterprise environments.

All splicing is performed by certified fusion splicer technicians and verified using OTDR testing, with full trace reports provided on completion. DWDM and passive optical network (PON) integration available.

Fusion Splicing

Precision joining of optical fibres with industry-leading splice loss <0.02dB. All splicers calibrated and maintained per manufacturer schedule.

OTDR Testing

Full OTDR traces with written reports for every run — bidirectional testing on long-haul OS2, event-by-event analysis provided in client handover pack.

Campus Backbone

Armoured outdoor direct-burial and aerial fibre between buildings and facilities — including duct installation, manholes and route survey.

DWDM & PON

Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing and Passive Optical Network integration — maximising capacity on existing fibre plant without additional duct work.
